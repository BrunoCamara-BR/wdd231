Ward Statistics

The name "Ward Statistics" was chosen because it clearly describes the purpose of the website, which is to display and analyze attendance statistics for a ward.

Site icon:
Domain name:
wardstatistic.org
Site Purpose:
Provide statistics about sacramental meeting attendance
Scenarios:
  • Which members attended the last sacramental meeting?
  • Which of those present at the sacramental meeting have low or zero frequency?
